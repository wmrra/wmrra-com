Contingency Programs

Motorcycle contingency is a performance-based rewards program offered by manufacturers and sponsors to racers. Riders who use eligible products and display required branding can earn cash, credits, or product awards based on their finishing position in a race.

Manufacturer Contingency

Many major motorcycle brands offer contingency programs for recent model bikes (1-2 years old).
You can learn more about qualifications and register directly on the manufacturers' websites.

  • BMW
  • Honda
  • Kawasaki
  • Yamaha

Local Sponsors & Services Contingency

Local brands and companies require you to fill out their contingency form before each race round. You can find the this season's contingency program participants and their forms below.

These forms must be filled out and submitted to contingency@wmrra.com before racing begins on Saturday in order to get paid.
If you do not submit these forms, you will NOT be eligible for contingency payments.
